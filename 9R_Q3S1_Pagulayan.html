Story

Final Space is a comedy drama television series created by Olan Rogers and developed by Rogers and David Sacks. The series follows an astronaut named Gary Goodspeed and his enormously powerful alien friend Mooncake on their intergalactic adventures as they attempt to save the universe from certain doom.








Characters



Garry Goodspeed


Gary Goodspeed is the son of John Goodspeed, an Infinity Guard captain who died while Gary was a child on a mission to close a breach to Final Space. Gary was abandoned by his mother, Sheryl Goodspeed, shortly after his father's death and forced into a life of crime. Years later, an emotionally stunted adult Gary impersonated an Infinity Guard soldier to impress officer Quinn Ergon, but accidentally destroyed 92 spaceships (plus a Mexican restaurant), and was sentenced to a 5-year prison sentence on the Galaxy One, where, on his final year of imprisonment, would meet and befriend a planet-destroying alien named Mooncake, and the Ventrexian bounty hunter Avocato, which subsequently puts him on a life-changing journey to unlock the mystery of Final

Avocato


One of Final Space's two tritagonists, along with Little Cato, is Avocato. He served as the Lord Commander's second-in-command and was a former Ventrexian general. He is capable, dangerous, and occasionally unreliable.

Quinn Ergon


A brilliant woman who discovers a terrifying breach in Time/Space, and when no one listens, she steals a spaceship and goes rogue to investigate. An Infinity Guard Cruiser captures, arrests, and kidnaps her before being sucked into the anomaly. Quinn escapes using a pod and is rescued by Gary. She then becomes one of the last good guys, fighting alongside the crew of the Galaxy One.

Mooncake


Mooncake. An anti-matter bomb explosion that shut down a doorway to Final Space left behind Mooncake, a planet-devouring spatial anomaly. Soon after, he was discovered aboard the Scarlet Lance near a supernova. He quickly escaped from the ship and ran into Gary Goodspeed, a prisoner being held by himself on the Galaxy One, who gave him the name Mooncake after a caterpillar he previously owned as a young child. In order to protect the universe from the Titans, the Lord Commander, and Invictus, all of whom want to use Mooncake in order to use Mooncake in order to achieve even more power and destroy the universe, Mooncake joins Gary (and the rapidly growing Team Squad).






Side Characters



Fox


Fox Polkawitz, a Tryvuulian who joined the Team Squad in Season 2 and perished in "One of Us," was a member of the squad. He was Clarence's adopted son as well as Ash Graven's adoptive sibling. A former soldier who was forced into battle and ran away from his past, Fox was known for being very hot-headed and childish, but was also shown to be very sensitive and gentle towards his friends, despite his imposing appearance.

Ash graven


Ash is an extremely powerful, young Serepentian woman whose powers first emerged after she was forced to watch the snake god Werthrent devour her sister, Harp Graven, during a sacrificial ceremony. In a fit of despair and rage, Ash's powers were activated in a powerful energy blast, which inadvertently killed her parents. Following the traumatic experience, Ash ran away from her home, where she met her new family, her adoptive brother Fox, and her adoptive father Clarence, before joining the Team Squad soon after.

Clarence


Clarence Polkawitz is a major character in the adult comedy series, Final Space, serving as a minor character in Season 1, a major antagonist in Season 2, and a minor, but pivotal protagonist in Season 3. He was a con artist and the father of Ash Graven and Fox.
